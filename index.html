Community standards

Safe & trusted community

📋

Terms agreed before sign-up

Every user must accept our Terms of Use and Community Guidelines before creating an account. The terms make clear that objectionable content and abusive behavior result in immediate removal.

🚩

Report objectionable content

Any post can be reported with a single tap. Reported content is instantly hidden from the reporting user's feed, and our team is notified immediately to review and take action.

🚫

Block abusive users

Blocking a user immediately removes all of their content from your feed, prevents any future contact, and notifies our moderation team so we can review the account for further action.
