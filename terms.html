Flair has a strict zero-tolerance policy for objectionable content and abusive behavior. The following are strictly prohibited:

  • Harassment, bullying, or threats of any kind

Violations may result in immediate account suspension or permanent removal without notice.

4. Reporting Objectionable Content

You may report any post you believe violates these guidelines using the report option available on every post (tap the ••• menu on any flight card). Reported content is immediately hidden from your feed. Our team is notified of every report and will review and take appropriate action, which may include content removal or account suspension.

5. Blocking Abusive Users

You may block any user from their profile page. Blocking a user immediately:

  • Removes all of their content from your feed
  • Prevents them from viewing your profile or content
  • Prevents any further interaction between your accounts
  • Notifies our moderation team so the account can be reviewed

Blocked users are not informed that they have been blocked.

6. Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time from Settings → Delete Account. Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. All your data, flights, and profile information will be deleted from our servers.
